Privacy Policy
📅 Last Updated: January 1, 2025

🔐 Our Core Privacy Commitment

Digital Will Locker is built on a zero-knowledge, offline-first architecture. All your data is stored locally on your device, encrypted with AES-256 military-grade encryption, and never transmitted to any server. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal information or vault contents.

1. Introduction

Digital Will Locker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles information. Because our App operates 100% offline, this policy is primarily about what we do NOT collect.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our App operates 100% offline. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Personal identification (name, email address, phone number)
  • Vault contents — documents, wills, PDFs, or images you store
  • Payment card or financial information stored in the vault
  • Emergency contact names or details
  • Passwords, master keys, or biometric data
  • 12-word recovery phrases
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Audio recordings from the AI voice assistant

3. Data Storage & Encryption

Every piece of data you enter is:

  • Stored exclusively on your local device — never on our servers
  • Encrypted using AES-256 military-grade encryption
  • Password-hashed using PBKDF2 with 10,000 iterations
  • Protected by your biometric lock (handled by your device's secure enclave)
  • Never synced to cloud services unless you explicitly export a backup

4. App Permissions & Why We Need Them

👆 Biometrics / Camera

For fingerprint and face ID authentication only. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave — we never access or store it.

💾 Storage / Files

To save your encrypted vault files and uploaded documents locally on your device only.

🎤 Microphone

Only used for the AI voice assistant feature. Audio processing follows Groq's privacy policy when using voice chat (opt-in only). No audio is stored by us.

🔔 Notifications

For Dead Man's Switch check-in reminders only. All notification logic runs locally on your device.

5. AI Features & Data

Digital Will Locker includes 8 AI-powered tools. These are designed to work offline-first. Any AI processing happens on-device. We do not send your data to any external AI service without your explicit opt-in.

If you choose to use the AI voice chat (powered by Groq's Whisper and LLaMA models), your voice input is processed per Groq's Privacy Policy. This feature is strictly opt-in only and requires an internet connection.

6. In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions (Weekly $3.99, Monthly $6.99, Yearly $41.99) are processed entirely through Google Play or Apple App Store. We never handle or store your payment information directly.

7. Dead Man's Switch

When the Dead Man's Switch activates, the App notifies your designated emergency contacts from your device directly. We do not have access to your emergency contacts or when notifications are sent.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
