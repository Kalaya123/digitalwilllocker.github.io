❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Is my data really stored offline only? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Digital Will Locker is 100% offline. All your data is stored exclusively on your device using AES-256 encryption. No data is ever sent to any server or cloud service. Your information never leaves your device.
What happens if I forget my master password? ▼
You can recover your vault using your 12-word recovery phrase provided when you created your vault. Store it safely in a physical location. Without both your password AND recovery phrase, access cannot be recovered — this is by design for maximum security.
How does the Dead Man's Switch work? ▼
The Dead Man's Switch monitors your app check-in activity. If you don't check in within a set time period (which you configure in Settings), it automatically notifies your designated emergency contacts and provides them with secure access instructions. You can customize the check-in interval from daily to weekly.
Can I use the app on multiple devices? ▼
Since the app is 100% offline, each device has its own separate vault. You can manually export and import your encrypted vault file to transfer between devices. Your subscription is tied to your Google Play or Apple App Store account and can be restored on the same account.
What encryption does the app use? ▼
The app uses AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit keys) — the same encryption used by governments and military worldwide. Your master password is hashed using PBKDF2 with 10,000 iterations, making brute-force attacks virtually impossible even with supercomputers.
How do I cancel my subscription? ▼
Subscriptions are managed through Google Play or Apple App Store. On Android: Google Play → Subscriptions → Digital Will Locker → Cancel. On iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Digital Will Locker → Cancel. Cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date.
How do I install the APK directly on Android? ▼
1. Download the APK file from the Download button on this page. 2. On your Android device go to Settings → Security → Enable "Install unknown apps" for your browser or file manager. 3. Open the downloaded APK file and tap Install. 4. The app will install and you can disable "unknown sources" again after.
What AI features are available in the app? ▼
The app includes 8 AI-powered tools: Emergency AI, Document Organizer AI, Home Remedies, Noise Detector, Mood Listener, Cost of Living tracker, AI Travel Guide, and AI Image Generator. The AI voice chat assistant (powered by Groq's Whisper & LLaMA) is also available and lets you speak to the AI using your microphone.
Is there a free version? What are the limits? ▼
Yes! Our Free Plan includes 20 MB secure storage, 1 emergency contact, AES-256 encryption, password protection, 12-word recovery phrase, and document upload — all with no time limit. Upgrade to Weekly ($3.99), Monthly Pro ($6.99), or Yearly Premium ($41.99) to unlock more storage and advanced features.
