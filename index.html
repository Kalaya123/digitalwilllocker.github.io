About Digital Will Locker

Digital Will Locker is created by an independent developer to help people securely store their important personal documents.

We focus on privacy, simplicity, and security for users worldwide. Unlike big tech companies, we believe your personal documents and digital will should never be stored on someone else's server. Your data belongs to you — and only you.

Every feature in Digital Will Locker is built with one goal in mind: give every person, regardless of technical background, a safe and simple way to protect what matters most — for themselves and for their loved ones.

We are a small, independent team with big values. No investors, no ads, no data selling — just a product built with care.
